Say hello to the Appleheads

Ever wonder what makes an apple an "applehead"? You've come to the right place! At Appleheads, we celebrate everything quirky, creative, and utterly delightful about these fantastic fruits. Get ready to peel back the layers and discover a bushel of characters and personalities, inspirational stories and fun facts that will surely make you an applehead, too!

Meet the family

John Applehead
Father, leader, and a beacon of strength and capability. His every effort is motivated by the deep love he holds for his family, and his absolute commitment ensures every job is meticulously done.

Jane Applehead
A woman whose love for her husband and children is her guiding light. Highly motivated, Jane pours her energy into cultivating a home that is not just warm and welcoming, but also a haven of love and safety. She tirelessly builds a foundation from which her family can flourish, grow, and pursue their individual paths with confidence.

Richard Applehead
Respected elder son idolizes his father and diligently following in his capable footsteps. More than just a learner, Richard is also his father's trusted "partner in crime," bringing enthusiasm and loyalty to every shared venture. His love for family and his drive to contribute reflect the very best of the Applehead spirit.

Simon Applehead
The younger son and much adored by his mother. Even as he finds his footing and charts his unique course, Simon's presence is defined by his warm heart, his wonderfully open nature, and a charismatic charm that's simply infectious. His imagination knows no bounds, promising a vibrant path ahead.
